Your avaliable commands:​

These commands adjust staff positions within the group.

  • /setrank [Username/ID] [Rank Name or ID] [Reason]
    • Moves a user directly to a specific rank.
    • Example: /setrank cookiedev7457 5 or /setrank cookiedev7457 Supervisor

More commands are unlocked as you progress through the groups ranks.

HR Ranking Guidelines​

  1. Audit Logs: Every command executed by Lynx Utilities is logged. Senior-High-Rank staff can audit these logs at any time to prevent abuse.
  2. Verification: Always verify the spelling of a username or the specific Rank ID before executing a direct setrank command.
  3. Permissions: If a command fails to execute, ensure your rank has the required permissions assigned within the Lynx Utilities dashboard.
  4. Bot Status: If the bot is unresponsive, wait 5-10 minutes, then try again, if it is still not working, please contact a Developer+.
