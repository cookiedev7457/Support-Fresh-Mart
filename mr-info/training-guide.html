Training Guide

info

The goal of this guide is to equip Store Managers+ with the protocols necessary to train, host and execute efficient training sessions for Lower Ranks.

  • Text in green italics represents the official script to be used by trainers when asked to by the host/co-host.
  • Text in bold contains specific instructional actions for the training staff.

Training Protocol and Conduct​

  • Trainers must maintain a professional pace; do not rush through the script to ensure LRs can comprehend all instructions.
  • Professionalism is mandatory. Any staff member engaging in maturity lapses or unprofessional behavior will be dismissed from the session and reported to Site Administration.
  • LRs are permitted a maximum of two AFK periods, not exceeding two minutes each. LRs must notify their trainer before going AFK.
  • Be direct and honest. Never falsely inform an LR they have failed as a joke; maintain a transparent evaluation process at all times.
  • The Host and Co-Host have the authority to remove excess MR/HR staff from the server if all training stations are occupied to prevent crowding.
  • Use the Fresh-Mart Discord for staff-to-staff communication during the session rather than in-game chat.

Session Rank Hierarchy​

SHR+ Overseeing

Please note, in the event of an Overseer, please continue the session as normal. They are there to supervise and are not to be spoken to unless asked a question.

Host and Co-Host Protocols​

Preparation and Claiming​

The Host/Co-host should arrive at the Training Center by XX:40 to verify the Announcer and ensure all trainers are present. At XX:45, the Host officially start's the session, and the Co-Host sends the first group shout.

Guide Verification​

Before the session begins, the Host or Co-Host must verify that all trainers are on the most updated version of this guide. Please use the line below as a format.

Host/Co-Host: Greetings! To ensure we are using the correct protocols today, please state the three green italicized lines found in your training guide.

Host/Co-Host: Thank you! Please note that you still use these lines whenever training.

Trainer Scripting​

The Introduction​

Conducted at the Cafe Station.

Trainer: Salutations! Welcome to your Fresh-Mart training session. I am (username), and I will be evaluating your performance today.

Trainer: If you need to step away (AFK), you must inform me first. You have a limit of two minutes, twice per session. Exceeding this will result in a kick.

Trainer: Should I disconnect, please remain at your station and wait for my return. Do not wander or join other groups.

Trainer: Please jump 2 times to indicate that you understand these rules and are ready to begin.

PAST THIS POINT IS IN DEVELOPMENT. DO NOT USE!

Phase 1: Cafe Evaluation​

Trainer: We will begin with the Test. Interaction and hospitality are key at Fresh-Mart.

Trainer: You must greet every customer politely. Your greeting must include the name "Fresh-Mart." For example: "Welcome to Fresh-Mart! How can I help you today?"

Trainer: To make a coffee, equip the white mug and use the dispenser. For slushies, equip the cold cup before dispensing.

Trainer: You must use the POS system to scan the items and ask the customer to scan their Cash Card to finalize.

Evaluation Criteria (Automatic Failures):

  • Failure to include "Fresh-Mart" in the greeting after one reminder.
  • Providing the incorrect item/order.
  • Lack of understandable English.
  • Unprofessionalism or rudeness.

Phase 2: Register Evaluation​

Trainer: Next is the Register Test. Follow me to the main checkout area.

Trainer: Log into the terminal by swiping your Staff Card. First, ask the customer for their Loyalty Card.

Trainer: Once the items appear, click "Finalize Transaction" and ask which payment method they prefer.

Trainer: End every interaction with a professional outro, wishing them a great day.

Trainer: Always remember to Sign Out of your register before leaving your post at the store.

Conclusion and Ranking​

Trainer: Congratulations, you have successfully passed your Fresh-Mart training!

Trainer: Please follow me to the ranking area. Remember to report any issues or trollers at the store to a Store Director+.

The Trainer should use the command /e :ff (username) on passing LRs and lead them to the green ranking line for the Host to process.

Post-Session

Once your LRs are at the line, trainers should exit the server promptly to allow the Host to finish the session without distractions.
