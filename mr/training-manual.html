Training Guide

Page In-development

This page is still in-development and is not 100% completed. Please do not use this script until asked to by your leading host.

info

The goal of this guide is to equip Store Managers+ with the protocols necessary to train, host and execute efficient training sessions for Lower Ranks.

  • Text in green italics represents the official script to be used by trainers when asked to by the host/co-host.
  • Text in bold contains specific instructional actions for the training staff.

Failure to do so, may result in ejection from the session

Training Protocol and Conduct​

  • Trainers must maintain a professional pace; do not rush through the script to ensure LRs can comprehend all instructions.
  • Professionalism is mandatory. Any staff member engaging in maturity lapses or unprofessional behavior will be dismissed from the session and reported to Site Administration.
  • LRs are permitted a maximum of two AFK periods, not exceeding two minutes each. LRs must notify their trainer before going AFK.
  • Be direct and honest. Never falsely inform an LR they have failed as a joke; maintain a transparent evaluation process at all times.
  • The Host and Co-Host have the authority to remove excess MR/HR staff from the server if all training stations are occupied to prevent crowding.
  • Use the Fresh-Mart Discord for staff-to-staff communication during the session rather than in-game chat.
